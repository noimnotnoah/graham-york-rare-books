I.About the Shop

A Devon bookshop trading since 1982

Graham York Rare Books has traded from 225 High Street, Honiton, since 1982. The shop deals generally in fine and rare books, with a stock of over twenty thousand volumes and particular collections in Spain, Gypsies, travel, local history, literature, theatre and music, art and architecture, and cookery, along with a particular leaning for the works of George Borrow.

Alongside the books, the shop stocks microscopes, stereoscopes, zograscopes, old maps, prints and ephemera, as well as bookcases. Graham York Rare Books is a member of the ABA (Antiquarian Booksellers' Association) and ILAB (International League of Antiquarian Booksellers), and issues mail-order catalogues alongside its shop stock.

In the Shop Today

Open Monday to Saturday for browsing, with earlier or later visits welcome by appointment. We do close from time to time for book fairs and buying trips further afield, so do ring ahead if you're travelling any distance.

The shop's mascot is Lottie.
